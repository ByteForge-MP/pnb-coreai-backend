TOPIC: PNB PROMOTION FROM GENERAL MANAGER TO CHIEF GENERAL MANAGER

SECTION: Eligibility Conditions
Must be serving General Manager in Scale VII.
Must have completed required minimum tenure as GM.
Must fall within the promotion consideration zone defined by HR.
Must have clean vigilance and integrity clearance.

SECTION: Performance Evaluation Criteria
Last 5 years APAR ratings should be Very Good or above.
Business performance of circle or vertical handled is evaluated.
NPA control and recovery track record is reviewed.
Profitability and growth indicators under leadership are considered.
Implementation of strategic or transformation initiatives is assessed.

SECTION: Leadership And Experience Factors
Experience in handling large circles or national verticals is preferred.
Exposure to corporate credit and high-value portfolios is important.
Head Office policy or strategic role experience is beneficial.
Ability to represent the bank in regulatory or consortium forums is valued.

SECTION: Selection Process
HR prepares eligible GM list based on seniority and service record.
Promotion committee evaluates performance, integrity, and leadership record.
Shortlisted candidates may be considered by the Board.
Final approval is subject to Board and Government clearance.

SECTION: Decision Authority
Promotion decided by Bank Promotion Committee.
Promotion approved by Board of Directors.
Government of India approval required for PSU banks.

SECTION: Outcome
Selected candidates are promoted to Scale VIII.
Promoted officers are assigned major circles, national verticals, or strategic head office responsibilities.
