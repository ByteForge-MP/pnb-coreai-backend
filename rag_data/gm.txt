The General Manager is responsible for leading large banking circles, national business verticals, or key head office divisions.
The role focuses on strategic planning, operational supervision, and large-scale business performance management.
A General Manager oversees major credit portfolios and monitors risk exposure across multiple zones or departments.

Promotion to Chief General Manager typically depends on seniority-cum-merit, performance record, vigilance clearance, and Board approval.
