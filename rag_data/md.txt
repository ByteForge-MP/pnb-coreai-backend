Shri Ashok Chandra assumed charge as the Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of Punjab National Bank on 16 January 2025.
Ashok Chandra has more than thirty-three years of experience in the banking industry across multiple leadership roles.

Before joining Punjab National Bank as Managing Director and CEO, Ashok Chandra served as Executive Director of Canara Bank.
He held the position of Executive Director in Canara Bank from 21 November 2022 to 15 January 2025.

During his tenure as Executive Director at Canara Bank, Ashok Chandra supervised several major banking verticals.
These verticals included Digital Banking and Information Technology, Strategy and Planning, Marketing and Public Relations, Financial Inclusion, MSME Banking, Retail Assets, Agriculture and Priority Sector Lending, Gold Loans, Liability Management, and General Administration.

Ashok Chandra also served as a Director on the Board of Canara HSBC Life Insurance Company Limited.
He was also a Director on the Board of PSB Alliance Limited.
In addition, he served as a Director on the Board of Canara Venture Capital Fund Limited.

Ashok Chandra is a member of the Indian Banks’ Association Standing Committee for Human Resources.

Before joining Canara Bank as Executive Director, Ashok Chandra worked as Chief General Manager at Union Bank of India.
As Chief General Manager at Union Bank of India, he was responsible for Recovery, Legal Affairs, and Stressed Asset Management.
During this period, he implemented strategic measures that significantly reduced stressed assets in the bank.
He was also a member of the Standing Committee on Stressed Assets established by the Indian Banks’ Association.

Ashok Chandra also served as a Director on the Board of The Union Trustee Company Private Limited.
He was also a Director of The National Asset Reconstruction Company Limited, commonly known as NARCL.

Ashok Chandra began his banking career with the erstwhile Corporation Bank on 10 September 1991 as a Probationary Officer.
During his career at Corporation Bank, he served as Branch Head in several regions across India.
He also handled leadership responsibilities by heading multiple zones and regional offices of the bank.

Ashok Chandra also gained international exposure when he headed the Representative Office of the bank in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

Throughout his banking career, Ashok Chandra received several awards and recognitions for leadership and performance.
These recognitions include Star Performer Award, Best Branch Award, Healthy Branch Award, Leadership Award, and Champion of Champions Award.

Ashok Chandra holds a Master’s Degree in Economics.
He is also a Certified Associate of the Indian Institute of Bankers.

Ashok Chandra was selected for a leadership development programme designed by the Banks Board Bureau.
This leadership programme was conducted by the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore during the period 2019 to 2020.
