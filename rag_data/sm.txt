PNB Scale III Senior Manager is a mid-level leadership position in the officer hierarchy of Punjab National Bank.
The Senior Manager role involves managing medium to large branches and supervising multiple operational areas.
A Scale III Senior Manager is responsible for ensuring strong business performance while maintaining regulatory and operational compliance within the branch.

The Senior Manager manages medium-sized or large branch operations within Punjab National Bank.
The role includes supervising officers, branch managers, and operational teams responsible for daily banking functions.
The officer approves higher-value retail and MSME loan proposals within delegated authority.
The Senior Manager monitors non-performing asset levels and ensures effective recovery actions for stressed accounts.
The officer drives business growth in deposits, advances, and fee-based income.
The position also requires ensuring audit compliance and maintaining operational risk control within the branch.

The Senior Manager has authority to approve moderate credit limits within delegated financial powers assigned by the bank.
The officer can recommend large corporate or consortium loan proposals to higher authorities for final approval.
The role carries responsibility for branch profitability and regulatory compliance performance.

The position requires advanced credit appraisal and financial risk assessment capability.
The Senior Manager must possess strong team leadership skills to manage branch employees effectively.
The role requires the ability to execute business strategies at the branch level and achieve performance targets.
The officer must maintain strong relationships with high-value customers and corporate clients.
Knowledge of banking regulations, audit procedures, and compliance frameworks is essential for this role.

The next promotion path after Senior Manager is Chief Manager, which is classified as a Scale IV leadership position in Punjab National Bank.
The typical promotion timeline for this role is approximately three to five years depending on performance, experience, and the bank’s promotion process.

A Scale III Senior Manager usually reports to the Regional Office or Zonal Office depending on the organizational structure.
The officer supervises Managers, junior officers, and operational staff responsible for branch operations.

Banks provide advanced credit and risk management programs to strengthen the professional capability of Senior Managers.
Training programs include leadership and team management workshops to develop managerial skills.
Officers also receive training in recovery strategies and non-performing asset control.
