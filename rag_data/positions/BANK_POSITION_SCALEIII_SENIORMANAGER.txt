TOPIC: PNB SCALE III SENIOR MANAGER POSITION

SECTION: Role Overview
Scale III Senior Manager is a mid-level leadership role responsible for managing larger branches, supervising multiple operational areas, handling higher-value credit proposals, and ensuring strong business performance while maintaining regulatory compliance.

SECTION: Key Responsibilities
Managing medium to large branch operations.
Supervising officers and branch managers.
Approving higher-value retail and MSME loans within delegated powers.
Monitoring NPA levels and recovery actions.
Driving business growth in deposits, advances, and fee income.
Ensuring audit compliance and operational risk control.

SECTION: Decision Authority
Can approve moderate credit limits within delegated authority.
Can recommend large corporate or consortium loans to higher authority.
Responsible for branch profitability and compliance performance.

SECTION: Skills Required
Advanced credit appraisal and risk assessment.
Team leadership and performance management.
Business strategy execution at branch level.
Customer relationship handling for high-value clients.
Regulatory and audit management capability.
