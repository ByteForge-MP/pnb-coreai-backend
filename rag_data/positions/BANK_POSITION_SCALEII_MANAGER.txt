TOPIC: PNB SCALE II MANAGER POSITION

SECTION: Role Overview
Scale II Manager is the first independent managerial position in the bank. Officers at this level supervise branch operations, manage staff, handle customer relationships, and take responsibility for business growth and compliance within their assigned unit.

SECTION: Key Responsibilities
Supervising branch staff and daily operations.
Approving small retail loans within delegated powers.
Ensuring KYC, AML, and RBI compliance.
Monitoring deposit growth and loan disbursement.
Handling customer grievances and escalations.
Supporting branch profitability targets.

SECTION: Decision Authority
Can approve small retail and MSME loans within limits.
Can recommend larger loans to higher authority.
Responsible for operational and compliance decisions in branch.

SECTION: Skills Required
Leadership and team supervision.
Retail and MSME credit understanding.
Customer relationship management.
Banking regulation and compliance knowledge.
Basic business development ability.
