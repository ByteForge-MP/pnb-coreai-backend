TOPIC: PNB SCALE I OFFICER POSITION

SECTION: Role Overview
Scale I Officer is the entry-level management position in the bank. Officers handle customer service, transactions, credit processing, and branch operations while learning banking systems and regulations.

SECTION: Key Responsibilities
Account opening and KYC verification.
Handling deposits, withdrawals, and customer queries.
Processing retail loans and documentation.
Assisting in branch business growth.
Compliance with RBI and bank policies.

SECTION: Decision Authority
Can recommend retail loans.
Cannot approve large credit independently.
Works under Branch Manager supervision.

SECTION: Skills Required
Banking regulations knowledge.
Customer service ability.
Basic credit analysis.
System and documentation handling.
