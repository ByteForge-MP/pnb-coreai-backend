TOPIC: PNB SCALE IV CHIEF MANAGER POSITION

SECTION: Role Overview
Scale IV Chief Manager is a senior managerial position responsible for handling large branches, overseeing key business functions, supervising multiple teams, and managing significant credit exposure while ensuring regulatory compliance and business profitability.

SECTION: Key Responsibilities
Leading large branches or specialized departments.
Supervising multiple managers and operational units.
Approving higher-value credit proposals within delegated limits.
Monitoring branch profitability, NPA control, and recovery.
Implementing bank policies and strategic initiatives.
Coordinating with regional and zonal offices for business planning.

SECTION: Decision Authority
Can approve substantial credit proposals within delegated authority.
Can recommend high-value corporate loans to higher authorities.
Responsible for business growth targets and operational risk control.

SECTION: Skills Required
Strong credit risk evaluation ability.
Strategic planning and execution.
Leadership across multiple teams.
Audit and compliance management.
Customer relationship handling for corporate clients.
