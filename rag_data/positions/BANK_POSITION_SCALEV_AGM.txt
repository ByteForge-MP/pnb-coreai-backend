TOPIC: PNB SCALE V ASSISTANT GENERAL MANAGER POSITION

SECTION: Role Overview
Scale V Assistant General Manager is a senior leadership role responsible for overseeing multiple branches, managing regional business performance, handling large credit proposals, and implementing strategic banking initiatives while ensuring compliance with regulatory and internal policies.

SECTION: Key Responsibilities
Supervising performance of multiple branches or functional departments.
Monitoring business growth in deposits, advances, and profitability.
Reviewing and recommending large credit proposals.
Ensuring effective NPA management and recovery strategies.
Implementing bank policies, digital initiatives, and operational reforms.
Coordinating with zonal office and head office for strategic execution.

SECTION: Decision Authority
Can approve large credit limits within delegated powers.
Can recommend major corporate credit proposals to higher authorities.
Responsible for regional business targets and operational efficiency.

SECTION: Skills Required
Strong corporate credit and risk analysis expertise.
Strategic leadership and regional business planning.
Decision-making under regulatory framework.
Team leadership across branches and departments.
Stakeholder and corporate relationship management.
